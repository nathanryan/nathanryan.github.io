About me

I'm a QA Engineer from Dublin, Ireland working with almost 10 years of experience in the field of software testing. I have a strong background in both manual and automated testing, and I am passionate about ensuring the quality and reliability of software products.

Throughout my career, I have worked on a wide range of projects across web and mobile applications. I mainly have experience in testing in the media industry (with The Irish Times and BBC) and through these roles I have worked on various types of testing, including functional, regression, performance, and security testing. I have also been involved in the development and maintenance of automated test suites using tools such as Playwright, Selenium and Appium. I am skilled in creating test plans, executing test cases, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software.
